The Hiring Process

Step 1

Apply

Complete and submit all required parts of the application. We invite all applicants to discuss how they have created positive change!

Step 2

Interview

After submitting your application, you will receive an email to schedule an interview with one of our staff at The Green Place!

Step 3

Decision

We will manually respond to every applicant. If we decide to hire you, you will receive an official job offer from us!
